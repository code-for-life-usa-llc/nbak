How To Make A Crossaint

  1. Pre-Heat the oven to 350 Degrees Ferenheit
  2. Roll up the dough into a cillindar shape
  3. Place the rolled dough in the oven for about 10-15 minutes
  4. After take the Crossaints out of the oven then smother butter on it with a butter knife
  5. Enjoy the tasty meal :)
